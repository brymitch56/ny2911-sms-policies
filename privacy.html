SMS Privacy Policy

Trail Life USA Troop NY-2911

Effective date: July 19, 2026

This policy describes how Trail Life Troop NY-2911 ("the Troop," "we") collects and uses mobile phone numbers in connection with our youth check-in and pickup notification program.

What we collect and why

Parents and legal guardians of enrolled youth may provide a mobile phone number on our signed consent and pickup-authorization form. We use that number for one purpose: to send SMS notifications related to the safety and pickup of their child — for example, a message when a youth is still checked in after a troop meeting or event has ended, and confirmations of replies to those messages.

We do not share mobile numbers

Mobile phone numbers and SMS opt-in information are not sold, rented, traded, or shared with any third parties or affiliates for any purpose, including marketing. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent are not shared with anyone. Numbers are stored on Troop-managed equipment, are accessible only to authorized Troop leadership, and are used solely to deliver the notifications described above through our SMS carrier (Twilio), which acts only as a message transmission provider.

Message frequency and rates

Message frequency varies with event attendance and is low — typically zero to a few messages per week, and only in connection with troop meetings and events your child attends. Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan.

Opting out

You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply START to re-enroll, or HELP for assistance. You may also contact Troop leadership to have your number removed. Opting out does not affect your child's enrollment.

Children's privacy

SMS messages are sent only to parents, guardians, and adults they have authorized in writing — never to youth members.

Changes and contact

If this policy changes, the current version will always be available at this address. Questions: contact Troop NY-2911 leadership at troopmaster2911@gmail.com.
