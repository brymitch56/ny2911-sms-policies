Trail Life Troop NY-2911 — Youth Pickup Notification Program

Effective date: July 19, 2026

1. Program description

Trail Life Troop NY-2911 sends SMS text messages to parents and legal guardians of enrolled youth who have opted in by providing their mobile number on our signed consent and pickup-authorization form. Messages relate to the safety and pickup of their child — for example, a notification when a youth remains checked in after a troop meeting or event has ended, and a confirmation when a reply is received.

2. Message frequency

Message frequency varies with event attendance and is low — typically zero to a few messages per week, sent only in connection with troop meetings and events.

3. Fees

The Troop does not charge for this service. Message and data rates may apply according to your mobile carrier and plan.

4. Opting out and help

Reply STOP to any message to cancel and receive no further messages. Reply START to re-enroll. Reply HELP for assistance, or contact troopmaster2911@gmail.com.

5. Supported carriers and liability

Messages are supported on major U.S. carriers. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages. Delivery is subject to effective transmission by your mobile carrier and is outside the Troop's control. SMS notifications are a convenience and do not replace the Troop's in-person youth check-in and release procedures.

6. Privacy

Mobile numbers and opt-in information are never sold, rented, or shared with third parties. See our SMS Privacy Policy.
